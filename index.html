About KT8D5

The Tatra KT8D5 is a three-section, bidirectional tram built by ÄŒKD Tatra from 1984 to 1999. It has been widely used in many cities in former Czechoslovakia and beyond. With a length of about 30.3 meters and capacity for over 200 passengers, it remains an iconic tram model.

Learn more on Wikipedia.

Our Volunteer Project

We are a group of volunteers in Bratislava working to restore an old KT8D5 tram to preserve it for future generations. Our goal is to repair, document, and bring this historical tram back to life.
